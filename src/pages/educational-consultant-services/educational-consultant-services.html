Special Education Services

At C.H.O.Y.C.E.S, we want to ensure that every student has the opportunity to equal access to education. That is why we provide specialized services to assist the student and integrate our services into the school curriculum.

Development and Understanding of IEP

IEP (Individualized Education Program) is a document developed by schools for children who are eligible for special education. We help you to understand the IEP process and guarantee that your child’s unique needs are addressed.

Curriculum Planning

Curriculum planning is the creation and structure of academic experiences. We will help to create a curriculum that is best suited for students, focus on important concepts, and plan out day-today activities, projects, and lessons.

Differentiated Instruction

At C.H.O.Y.C.E.S, we understand that every student’s learning techniques are different and every student’s curriculum begins with students’ interest and experiences. To ensure every student’s needs are met, we individualize our tutoring programs based on areas that need improvement. Tutors will work with students individually at a pace that is comfortable for them to strengthen their skills.

Data Driven Instruction

This process is using data that is gathered and analyzed about students in the classroom and is utilized to improve how students are taught in order to enhance student performance throughout the year. C.H.O.Y.C.E.S is here to help integrate data driven instruction in your classroom!

Teacher Observation and APPR

APPR is an evaluation of teacher performance through the use of student achievement and growth. We will help with the APPR and ensure you are doing what it takes to score high on APPR.

Response to Intervention

This is a method used to help students improve on skills or topics they are struggling with. We will use test scores and assignments to evaluate the student and focus heavily on the area of weakness. Students will be monitored closely to measure progress.

Resume Writing

Having a well-written resume is important to gain the attention of hiring managers. C.H.O.Y.C.E.S will provide services to help improve your resume to accentuate your education, work experience, credentials, and accomplishments.
