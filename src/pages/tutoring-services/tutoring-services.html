Tutoring Services

Academics

We offer tutoring in all major subjects including Math, English Language Arts, Reading, Writing, Science and Social studies through K-12.

Test Preparation

SAT Subject

Services are available to assist students to prepare for individual SAT subject tests to improve skills in specific subjects. The sessions take place over the course of 6 hours.

SAT / ACT

With our SAT/ACT programs, we will assist students to prepare for these exams or to raise their scores in order to help them feel more confident and prepared for test day. The sessions take place over the course of 19.5 hours.

PSAT

Services are available to assist students with the PSAT, an exam taken prior to the SAT. Many of the sessions are over the course of 16.5 hours.

College Readiness

Application

We provide assistance with every step of the college application and essay process to ensure that deadlines are met and are done efficiently and effectively.

Mentorship

We guide each student to ensure that they are taking the correct classes in high school, developing important skills, and exploring extracurricular activities/ volunteer opportunities.

College Research

Finding the correct college for each student is an important, but difficult decision. We provide assistance in finding the one that is the right fit and provides the best opportunities.
